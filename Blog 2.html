My Journey as a Team Leader: Challenges, Triumphs, and Lessons Learned

Published by Joaquin Aaron Recio on April 19, 2024

Leadership can manifest in many ways, especially when it comes to group or team dynamics. My journey as a leader has taken me through various roles and experiences, each with its unique challenges and rewards.

Leading in Different Capacities

I’ve had the privilege of serving as a class representative for my class section, leading a project team for our capstone project, and even acting as a household leader in my Catholic community. Our capstone project was an engaging mental health stress game that was responsive, and we successfully completed three website and two mobile app projects.

The Challenges of Leadership

One of the most challenging aspects of being a leader is managing time. We all have our own priorities in life, and it’s not always possible to control the time of others. Many leaders, including myself, struggle with self-doubt, also known as impostor syndrome. Handling difficult people in your organization and work life can also be challenging.

Another common challenge is scope creep, which occurs when new requirements are added to a project that were not initially agreed upon. This can be difficult for a leader as it often necessitates changes to the project.

Overcoming Personal Weaknesses

My greatest weakness has always been poor communication. This is compounded by the fact that I have an autism spectrum disorder. However, since my diagnosis, I’ve been doing my best to step up and try something new for myself, striving to become a better version of myself.

Solving Problems and Overcoming Challenges

Time management is one of the most challenging things that we may encounter, not only in the community but also in life. I’ve managed to overcome several challenges, including time management, overcoming self-doubt, handling difficult people and resolving conflicts, managing scope creep, and improving communication skills over time.

The Rewards of Leadership

The best part of being a leader, for me, is the opportunity to leverage my networking skills to foster strong social connections with others. It allows me to get to know them, understand their insights, and share thoughts that can contribute to the community. It also enables me to create some of the best memories with them.

In conclusion, my journey as a team leader has been filled with challenges and triumphs. It has taught me valuable lessons about leadership, teamwork, and personal growth. As I continue on this path, I look forward to learning more and making a positive impact on the teams and communities I serve.
